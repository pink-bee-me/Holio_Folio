...let's talk about me!


Code is like artwork..just as art has a wide range of variety of content, style and artisan skill-level -- it is also that way with computer code. Code can be a rare and brilliant masterpiece,the simplest of doodles,or anything in between.That's the beauty of it . Code is a creation--brought to life by the artist...the computer programmer. The discovery, the journey, the trials and tribulations, obstacles overcome, and the never-ending hunt for buried treasure keep things exciting... Critical thinking is a skill and an art form and the the foundation of great code. I have always enjoyed fixing things that are broken, finding solutions to problems, helping others, and making people smile. My goal is to accomplish these things through code.
